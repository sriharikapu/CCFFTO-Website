About

The Toronto Creative Commons Film Festival (CCFF.TO) will take place in September and October throughout Toronto, Canada. CCFF.TO will be screening audiovisual works created with Creative Commons Licenses. Our aim is to promote dialogue around open access media with screenings in several locations and theatres throughout the city. CCFF.TO is a member of CCWorld; CCWorld is a net of festivals around the world whose aim is to share, extend and promote audiovisual works that follow openness and free culture criteria. A network of free adhesion and peer collaboration on a global scale for local action and transformation.
